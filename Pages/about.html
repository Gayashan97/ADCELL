About ADCELL

ADCELL offers readers a concise, current and comprehensive overview of the latest scientific research in all fields and applications of science and technology.

ADCELL is edited for an educated readership of professionals, scientists and other science enthusiasts. ADCELL also provides an store of science items, Science images and Quizes for users. Taken from articles of sciencenews.org written by a staff of experienced science journalists, it treats science as news, reporting accurately and placing findings in perspective.

Published since 2019, Updated continuously online,

ADCELL is published by the Web Development & Designing Team of InfoSchol Batch of 2018, Informatics Institute of Technology, Colombo 06.
